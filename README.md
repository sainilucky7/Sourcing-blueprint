# Sourcing-blueprint
1. Competitor & Tier-1 Semiconductor Companies: Intel, AMD, NVIDIA, TSMC, Broadcom, Qualcomm
2. Open-Source Hardware & FPGA Communities: GitHub repositories, Contributors to open-source EDA , Forums like EDAboard, Reddit FPGA/ASIC subs, and Hackaday
3. Research Papers & Technical Publications: IEEE journals (Solid-State Circuits, VLSI, Microelectronics), Conference papers: Hot Chips Symposium, ISSCC, DAC, ICCAD, ArXiv preprints in semiconductor or VLSI
4. University Research Labs & Postdocs: MIT, Stanford, UC Berkeley, Georgia Tech, EPFL, Labs focused on VLSI, chip design, analog/digital systems, FPGA, AI accelerators
5. Patent Inventor Searches: USPTO, Google Patents
6. Professional Associations & Technical Communities: IEEE Solid-State Circuits Society, ACM SIGDA, VLSI technical forums, LinkedIn hardware groups
7. LinkedIn Boolean Search / Advanced Filters

# Use a combination of title, skills, and company filters:
8. Kaggle & ML Accelerator Programs (for AI-SoC roles): Engineers working on AI chips, accelerators, or hardware-aware ML frameworks, Look at competition winners or contributors in embedded AI
9. Internal Employee Referrals & Alumni Networks
